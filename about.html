About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to helping farmers and plant enthusiasts detect and prevent leaf diseases early using modern AI technology.

Our mission is simple: make advanced plant health monitoring accessible to everyone — from small-scale farmers in rural areas to home gardeners and large agricultural businesses.

By combining powerful machine learning models with an easy-to-use interface, we aim to reduce crop losses, minimize pesticide usage, and promote sustainable farming practices.
